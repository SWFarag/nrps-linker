* NRPS-Linker processes non-ribosomal peptide biosynthetic gene clusters after being identified using the AntiSMASH tool. In the process of scanning and identifying NRP BGCs, AntiSMASH uses three algorithms to predict the specificities of NRPS adenylation domain (Minowa .et.al, Roettig, M et.al, Stachelhaus et.al). If all three algorithms agree on the same predicated subtrate then it will be used as the substrate of choice by AntiSMASH, otehrwise, AntiSMASH uses the term 'nrp' for all unidentified NRPS substrates. Hence, there are two columns of IMLs one were 'nrp' containing IMLs are excluded and the other one were they are included.
